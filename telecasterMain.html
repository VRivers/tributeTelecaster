The History

The start of the Electric Guitar sound

  • 1938 - The company began as Fender's Radio Service in Fullerton, California.
  • 1950 - Fender introduced the first mass-produced solid-body electric guitar, the Telecaster ("Tele")
  • 1965 - Leo Fender sold his companies to the Columbia Broadcasting System (CBS) for $13 million.
  • 1985 - the Company employees purchased the company from CBS and renamed it Fender Musical Instruments Corporation (FMIC).
